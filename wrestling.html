My experience

Beginning from the summer of my 8th grade I began wrestling. At first, I progressed slowly, but with persistance I started to learn. I started on the varsity team my freshman year, where I ended with a losing record. In the offseason, I trained at CYC, allowing me to end my sophmore season with a record of 22-10. That following off-season I joined the CYC elite program, where I furthered my wrestling knowledge of Greco-Roman wrestling, eventually allowing me to end my junior year with a record of 31-9 and HM ranking in the state .
